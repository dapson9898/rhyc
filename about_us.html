VISION STATEMENT

To build a united, empowered, and politically conscious generation of Nigerian youth who are actively engaged in governance, promoting national unity, and driving the continuity of the Renewed Hope Agenda through purposeful participation and service to the nation.

MISSION STATEMENT

To mobilize and inspire Nigerian youth to take an active role in governance and democracy by bridging the gap between the youth and government, advocating for youth interests, and supporting the re-election of the President to sustain the progress, stability, and transformation of Nigeria under the Renewed Hope administration.
